About Me

I have worked in manufacturing environments for the last 18 years. I've held roles as CNC machine operators, technical quality focused roles for mechanical centric departments, and plant wide quality support in Engineering roles.

I have spent most of my career in the Printed Circuit Board industy and three years in the plastic machine and fabrication industy where I was a Manufacturing Engineer.

I realized I wanted to change my careers as I was tapped out in the engineering . tech / manufacturing engineering space. I taught myself to program starting with Python and utilizing my companies Microsoft SQL server to build an in house ERP app that filled software gaps within the company.

After teaching myself some programming I started taking classes at Portland Community College eventually transferring to Portland Statue Univeristy. In this time I moved into a Software Engineering role where I develop code for Oracle at my current company and have been doing this job for just over Three years.
